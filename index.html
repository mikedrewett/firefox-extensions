Steam Cards Desktop

Scans your Steam wishlist to detect which games have trading cards, then overlays card prices and sale information directly on the wishlist page. Requires the Steam Cards desktop app, which handles data syncing and price tracking in the background.

Version 1.0.0 Requires desktop app · MV3
